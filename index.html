2′-Modified RNA Duplex Stability Calculator

Predict sequence-dependent ΔH°, ΔS°, and ΔG°37 values for RNA/RNA, RNA/DNA, and four 2′-modified RNA/RNA duplexes under non-crowding and cell-like conditions.

Conditions: non-crowding · cell-like

Sequence Input

1

Sequence input. Enter Strand A and Strand B below. Strand B can be generated automatically.
Accepted notation: RNA, A/C/G/U; DNA, dA/dC/dG/dT; 2′-OMe, Ao/Co/Go/Uo; 2′-F, Af/Cf/Gf/Uf; 2′-MOE, Am/Cm/Gm/Um; LNA, Al/Cl/Gl/Ul.

2

Mode selection. Choose the duplex type and solution condition. The selected chemistry determines the required notation for Strand B.

The self-complementary correction is applied automatically only when a strand is identical to its own reverse-complement sequence.

3

Execution and output. Click Calculate to obtain ΔH°, ΔS°, ΔG°37, and the detailed NN contribution breakdown.
