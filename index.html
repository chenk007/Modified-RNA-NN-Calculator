Sequence input

Strand 1 is unmodified RNA written 5′→3′. Strand 2 is written 3′→5′ and uses the chemistry selected above. Spaces, hyphens and terminal labels are ignored.

The self-complementary correction is applied automatically only when Strand 1 is identical to its own reverse-complement sequence, for example 5′-AACCGGUU-3′ for RNA or 5′-AACCGGTT-3′ for DNA.
